Delivery

The time taken for the product to reach your doorstep varies according to where you are.

  • For West Malaysia, it takes up to 4 days.
  • For East Malaysia, it takes up to 7 days.

However, if there is an unexpected delay with the courier, a voucher will be send to your email. Apologies for the inconvenience.

Already placed an order?

Once your parcel has been dispatched from our warehouse you will receive a confirmation e-mail with a tracking number, which can be used on the J&T' website to get further updates. Please check your spam folder if you are having difficulties locating this e-mail.

Visit our delivery partner's website,J&T for latest updates on possible delays in your local area.

Refund

How do I return or exchange my order?

We have a 15-day return policy, which means you have 15 days after receiving your item to request a return.

Let us know if you prefer a refund or exchange.

Refund will be made via bank transfer to the bank of your choosing.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging. You'll also need the receipt or proof of purchase.

In the event that a return needs to be made, contact us within 7 days of delivery (including weekends) and we'll furnish you with the necessary details required for the return. Do note that returns should be shipped back within 14 days of delivery (including weekends), and that buyers will have to bear the return shipping costs. Items sent back to us without first requesting a return will not be accepted.

You can always contact us for any return question.
